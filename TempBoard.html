Custom Tempreture Board

This project was specifically developed for the Northwestern Formula SAE 2023 electric car. With the transition to an all-electric vehicle this year, it became crucial to devise a reliable temperature monitoring system to provide real-time data to both the driver and the team. This board utilizes the ESP32 microcontroller, which efficiently records and transmits information through the CANBUS protocol. To accurately capture the ambient temperature at various points on the car, two NTC thermistors have been strategically placed. Additionally, a waterproof NTC thermistor is incorporated to precisely measure the coolant temperature. The board is further equipped with a flow rate sensor to monitor the coolant flow.
